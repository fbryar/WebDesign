Interior design is key to making a house feel a certain way whether that be warm and cozy

or sleek and modern. Choosing the right furniture and having and understanding of color are

all parts of making a space.

Another part of designing a room is choosing the right furniture. You have to make sure that the furniture your choose goes with the overall design and doesnt feel out of place

Balance in interior design is very important because it allows the room to not feel to cramped or empty, finding the right balance can vastly improve a room
